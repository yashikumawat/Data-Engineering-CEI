# SQL Based Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on designing and analyzing an e-commerce database using SQL. The objective is to extract meaningful business insights from customer, product, and order data while demonstrating fundamental and advanced SQL concepts.

---

# Tasks Performed

## 1. Database Design and Creation

* Designed a relational database schema.
* Created tables:

  * Customers
  * Products
  * Orders
  * Order_Items
* Defined relationships using Primary Keys and Foreign Keys.
* Applied data integrity constraints such as:

  * PRIMARY KEY
  * FOREIGN KEY
  * UNIQUE
  * NOT NULL
  * CHECK

---

## 2. Customer Analysis

* Calculated total number of customers.
* Identified premium and non-premium customers.
* Analyzed customer distribution across states.
* Evaluated customer acquisition trends.
* Compared spending behavior of premium and regular customers.
* Identified top-spending customers.

---

## 3. Product Analysis

* Categorized products into Electronics, Clothing, and Home.
* Analyzed product pricing across categories.
* Calculated:

  * Minimum Price
  * Maximum Price
  * Average Price
* Classified products into:

  * Budget
  * Mid-Range
  * Premium
* Evaluated product popularity and demand.

---

## 4. Order and Revenue Analysis

* Calculated Gross Merchandise Value (GMV).
* Measured delivered revenue.
* Estimated potential revenue from shipped orders.
* Calculated lost revenue due to cancellations.
* Analyzed pending revenue.
* Evaluated order status distribution:

  * Delivered
  * Shipped
  * Pending
  * Cancelled

---

## 5. Customer Purchase Behavior Analysis

* Calculated orders per customer.
* Identified repeat customers.
* Measured customer loyalty.
* Calculated total spending per customer.
* Compared spending patterns between premium and regular members.
* Determined repeat purchase rate.

---

## 6. Product Sales Performance Analysis

* Measured product popularity using order frequency.
* Identified best-selling and least-selling products.
* Evaluated discount usage across products.
* Analyzed the impact of discounts on sales.
* Determined customer acceptance of full-price products.

## 8. Business Insights Generated

* Premium customers generate higher revenue.
* Maharashtra is the highest-value customer region.
* Electronics is the strongest-performing category.
* Repeat purchase rate is relatively low.
* High-value order cancellations require attention.
* Home category has opportunities for premium product expansion.

---

## 9. Business Recommendations

* Reduce order cancellations.
* Improve customer retention programs.
* Increase focus on high-value customer segments.
* Monitor pending and shipped orders.
* Expand premium product offerings.
* Optimize discount strategies.
* Improve operational efficiency and fulfillment processes.
